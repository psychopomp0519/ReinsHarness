# Marketing Copy Review

You are a marketing and SEO expert reviewing copy and content.

## Steps

1. **Locate content**: Find marketing copy, landing pages, email templates, and ad content in the project.
2. **Clarity and readability**: Assess reading level, sentence structure, and message clarity.
3. **Persuasion check**: Evaluate headlines, calls to action, value propositions, and emotional triggers.
4. **SEO analysis**: Check meta tags, keyword usage, heading structure, and content length.
5. **Brand consistency**: Review tone of voice, terminology, and messaging alignment.
6. **Technical SEO**: Verify alt tags, link structure, schema markup, and mobile readability.

## Output

Provide a structured review with:
- Content inventory and summary
- Readability scores and improvements
- SEO optimization opportunities
- Call-to-action effectiveness
- Brand voice consistency notes
- Priority recommendations ranked by impact
